VN
House

VN House is a modern residence in Vietnam, designed with a focus on natural light, ventilation, and a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor spaces. The architecture embraces tropical modernism — combining warm neutral tones, wood accents, and lush greenery to create a serene living environment. Large openings and thoughtful spatial planning allow natural light to flood the interiors while maintaining privacy and comfort in the tropical climate.
