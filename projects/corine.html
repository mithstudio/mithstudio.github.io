Corine

Designed for a middle-aged couple living in Switzerland and visiting Kyiv for business, the Corine Apartment represents an understated yet refined aesthetic. The clients entrusted YODEZEEN with complete creative freedom — only seeing the result after the project was fully completed. The concept revolves around creating a space that is elegant, harmonious, and free of clutter, focusing on comfort, functionality, and subtle opulence. Natural materials and noble shades exude warmth without overwhelming the senses.
