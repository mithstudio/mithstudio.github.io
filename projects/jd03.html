JD03

The JD03 project is a modern interior design execution located in the 'DOCK32' residential complex in Kyiv, Ukraine. The apartment features a compact and highly optimized footprint of 41 square meters. Based on the visual assets and the studio's profile, the project showcases LEMON Interior Design's ability to maximize functionality and style in smart-sized living spaces. The design incorporates a cohesive open-plan living area, a comfortable private bedroom, a dedicated wardrobe/dressing space, and a modern bathroom.
