FP08

The FP08 project is a modern interior design execution located in the 'Prestige Hall' (ЖК Престиж-хол) residential complex in Kyiv, Ukraine. The apartment spans a total area of 105 square meters. Based on the company's service offerings and the layout inferred from the images, the project showcases LEMON Interior Design's turnkey renovation and design capabilities. The space is comprehensively zoned into a large kitchen and living area, multiple bedrooms (including a primary suite and a secondary bedroom), a dedicated wardrobe/dressing room, and several bathrooms/WCs.
