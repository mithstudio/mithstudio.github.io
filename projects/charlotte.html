This project is a high-end 3D architectural visualization of a modern residential house. The focus was on achieving photorealistic lighting, natural material response, and a seamless connection between interior and exterior spaces. The environment integrates lush greenery and soft daylight to enhance realism and atmosphere. Every element—from composition to post-production—was carefully crafted to deliver a premium visual experience.
