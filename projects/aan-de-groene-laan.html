We are proud of the visualizations and animation we have provided for the Nieuw Kralingen sub-area 'Aan de Groene Laan', an exclusive residential project in Rotterdam. For this project we created both interior and exterior renders to give future residents a realistic impression of their new home. We have also created an animation that brings the homes to life. The design combines contemporary elegance with warm, natural materials — creating a living experience that is both luxurious and inviting.
